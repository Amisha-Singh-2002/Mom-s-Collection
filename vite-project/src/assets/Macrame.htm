Description

Small macrame wall hanging for an otherwise boring wall! The product is smaller in size yet the detailing is at par. Dangle it alone or combine it with wall pieces to complete your own creative space.

Material : 100% Organic Cotton Thread Colour : Turquoise Measurements : 9" width by 12" length Product weight : 300 grams Special Attention : Handcrafted, Please Allow For Minor Crafting Variation. Shipping Info : Dispatched in a maximum of 7-21 business days. Returns accepted within 12 days of delivery. Craft : Macramé (Macramé is a form of textile produced using knotting (rather than weaving or knitting) techniques. It was very popular back in the 1970’s with the hippie culture, but it has come back into fashion as part of the recent tribal and Boho (Bohemian) design trends in home decor and fashion accessories.) About the impact, location and community details: We are a small group of woman artisans based in a small town of Banswara, in Rajasthan. Our motto is to teach this craft to as many women as possible and provide them with an extra source of income while creating beautiful products for our beloved patrons. Product Care: Gentle hand-wash.
